## PREAMBLE

A doctrine written against gatekeeping cannot be released under a license that quietly reintroduces one. Standard licenses were built to protect institutions, publishers, and corporations from liability and from each other — they were not built to protect the *independent mind* the OSJ exists to defend. The OSJ License is therefore its own instrument: short enough to read in one sitting, and structured so that no future Gatekeeper — commercial, academic, or algorithmic — can fence this work back behind a wall.
